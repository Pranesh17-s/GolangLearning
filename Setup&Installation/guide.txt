1. Open any Browser.
2. Search Go install
3. In the results look for go.dev/docs/install
4. Visit that site and Select your OS and click download
5. This will navigate you to go.dev/dl/
6. In this site download Go for your OS and click the download button
7. Open the exe after it's downloaded and follow the instructions in it.
8. After installing verify by opening your command prompt and type go. This will list some commands if Go is installed without issue.
